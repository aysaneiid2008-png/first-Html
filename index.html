New york city - Statue of Liberty

A brief explanation of Statue of Liberty in New York.

The Statue of Liberty is located on Liberty Island,New York Harbor, in New York City.This statue was a gift from the people of France to the United States of America.

The height of the Statue of Liberty (United States) is 93 meters. This height includes the base of the statue and the statue itself. The height of the statue itself without the base is 46 meters. The Statue of Liberty was installed in 1886 , so it is 139 years old this year (2025) . 2.3 million people visit the Statue of Liberty every year,and because of people's great interest in this statue, various countries, including Brazil, Pakistan, Malaysia, Taiwan, and even China, have built a copy of the Statue of Liberty in their countries!

One of Frisch-Auguste Bartholdi's most prominent statements about the character of freedom, which well illustrates her perspective, is this:

I hate any superfluous detail in sculpture. The forms and works of this art should be broad, massive, and simple.

And he chose a lamp (torch) to be a symbol that was clearly understandable from a distance, without complex details that would be lost in the distance.

The part of Liberty Island (the island where the Statue of Liberty is located) that is underwater is part of the state of New Jersey , not New York . The original name of Liberty Island (where the Statue of Liberty is located) is Bedloe's Island.
